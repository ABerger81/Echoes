# Escape System

## Purpose

Transform exploration into panic.

---

# Trigger

Player acquires treasure.

---

# Escape Loop

Treasure Found
↓
Monster Activated
↓
Player Escapes
↓
Success or Failure

---

# Success

Player reaches exit.

Rewards:

- Score Saved
- Leaderboard Entry

---

# Failure

Monster catches player.

Penalties:

- Reduced Score
- Game Over
