# Heartbeat System

## Purpose

Create tension and communicate danger.

---

# Design Goals

- Increase immersion
- Signal danger
- Affect player perception

---

# States

## Calm

Heartbeat: 60 BPM

Effects:

- No visual distortion

---

## Alert

Heartbeat: 90 BPM

Effects:

- Audible heartbeat

---

## Fear

Heartbeat: 120 BPM

Effects:

- Peripheral darkening

---

## Panic

Heartbeat: 150 BPM

Effects:

- Heavy breathing
- Reduced situational awareness

---
