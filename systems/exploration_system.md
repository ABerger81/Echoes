# Exploration System

## Purpose

Support discovery and learning.

---

# Player Goals

- Navigate
- Observe
- Learn
- Find Treasure

---

# Core Principles

- Curiosity drives progression
- Information is reward
- Knowledge becomes skill

---

# Exploration Loop

Explore
↓
Observe
↓
Learn
↓
Discover Clue
↓
Continue Exploration
