# Clue System

## Purpose

Teach players how to survive.

---

# Design Philosophy

Clues should reward attention.

Players should become more skilled through knowledge.

---

# Example

## Minotaur

Player Learns:

"The Minotaur follows blood trails."

Escape Phase:

Blood trails become meaningful.

---

# Requirements

- Clues must be visible
- Clues must be understandable
- Clues must become useful later
