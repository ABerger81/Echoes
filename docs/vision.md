# Vision

## Core Fantasy

The player is a Victorian-era explorer entering dangerous mythological locations in search of treasure.

The player cannot defeat the monsters guarding these treasures.

The only options are:

* Explore
* Learn
* Hide
* Escape

---

## Core Pillars

### Exploration

Discover forgotten places and learn their secrets.

### Tension

Fear is created through anticipation and atmosphere.

### Escape

Finding treasure transforms the player from explorer into prey.

---

## Long-Term Vision

A world containing multiple mythological regions, each featuring unique creatures, environments, clues, and escape mechanics.
