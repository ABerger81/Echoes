# Lore

## Purpose

Contains story elements.

Lore should support gameplay.

Lore should never dictate gameplay.

---

# World Premise

The player is an adventurer seeking ancient treasures hidden in forgotten mythological locations.

Many stories thought to be myths are real.

---

# Narrative Themes

- Greed
- Curiosity
- Fear
- Discovery
- Survival

---

# Open Questions

- Why do these creatures guard treasure?
- Who built these locations?
- Are the myths connected?
